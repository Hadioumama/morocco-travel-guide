Discover the Most Beautiful Destinations

Welcome to Morocco Tourism Guide, your resource for exploring the wonders of the Kingdom of Morocco

TANGIER

Morocco's fabled port where Africa meets Europe. A city of layered history, from its ancient medina to its cosmopolitan corniche, forever washed by two seas.

MARRAKECH

Marrakech is the beating heart of Morocco, a city where ancient traditions vibrate beneath a modern energy. Known as the "Red City" for its distinctive rose-hued walls and buildings, it's a place of intoxicating sensory overload.

CASABLANCA

Casablanca, Morocco's economic hub, blends modern skyscrapers and Art Deco architecture with historic quarters. Its iconic landmark is the Hassan II Mosque, overlooking the Atlantic. A dynamic, cosmopolitan port city..

MERZOUGA

The village itself is a simple, rustic outpost, but its true magic lies in the surrounding desert. It is the prime destination for unforgettable experiences such as camel treks into the dunes, spending a night under a blanket of stars in a desert camp, and witnessing spectacular sunrises and sunsets over the vast, silent sands..
